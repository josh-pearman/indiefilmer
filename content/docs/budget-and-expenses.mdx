## Budget Buckets

Your budget is organized into customizable buckets:

- **Locations** — location rental and fees
- **Food** — catering and crafty
- **Gear** — equipment rental and purchases
- **Talent** — cast day rates
- **Crew** — crew day rates
- **Post-Production** — editing, color, sound
- **Miscellaneous** — everything else

Add your own buckets or rename the defaults to match your production's needs.

## Automatic Roll-Up

Costs from other sections automatically flow into the budget:

- Location costs → Locations bucket
- Catering costs → Food bucket
- Active gear scenario → Gear bucket
- Cast day rates → Talent bucket
- Crew day rates → Crew bucket

This means your budget always reflects the latest data from across your project.

## Logging Expenses

Log individual expenses against any budget bucket. Each expense has an amount, description, date, and optional receipt attachment. Expenses represent actual money spent.

## Planned vs. Actual

Each bucket shows two numbers: **planned** (estimated costs from your production data) and **actual** (logged expenses). This gives you a clear picture of where you're on budget and where you're running over.
