## Shoot Days

The schedule page shows all your shoot days in chronological order. Each shoot day has:

- **Date** — when the shoot takes place
- **Location** — where you're shooting
- **Call time** — general crew call time
- **Status** — Planned, Shooting, or Wrapped

Click "Add Shoot Day" to create a new day, then fill in the details. You can also click any existing day to view and edit it.

## Assigning Scenes

From a shoot day's detail page, you can assign scenes to that day. Scenes are pulled from your scene breakdown — you'll see a list of unscheduled scenes you can add. A single scene can be assigned to multiple shoot days if needed (for example, if a scene spans two shooting days).

## Day Status

Each shoot day progresses through three statuses:

- **Planned** — still in prep
- **Shooting** — currently in production
- **Wrapped** — completed

Update the status as your production progresses to keep everyone informed.

## Reordering Scenes

Within a shoot day, you can drag scenes to reorder them. The order represents your planned shooting order for that day — shoot the master first, then coverage, etc.

## Generating Call Sheets

From any shoot day, click "Generate Call Sheet" to create a printable call sheet. The call sheet automatically includes the general call time, weather, location details, assigned crew and cast with their individual call times, the scene breakdown, and any announcements. Call sheets can be printed or exported as PDF.
