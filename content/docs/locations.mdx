## Location List

Manage all your shooting locations in one place. Each location has a name, address, and status. Link to Peerspace, Giggster, or any other venue provider for easy reference.

## Venue Options

For each location, you can add multiple venue options. This is useful during scouting — compare different apartments for the "protagonist's home" or different cafes for the "diner scene." Each venue has its own address, cost estimate, photos, and notes. Select one venue as the preferred option when you've made your choice.

## Photos & Files

Upload scout photos and reference files directly to locations or individual venues. Photos are displayed in a gallery view on the location detail page, making it easy to review options with your team.

## Cost Tracking

Set estimated and actual costs per location. Costs automatically roll up into the Budget section under the "Locations" bucket. Track deposits, rental fees, and any additional charges.
