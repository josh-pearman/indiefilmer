## Gear Scenarios

Gear is organized into **scenarios** — think of them as different equipment packages you're considering. For example:

- "Budget Package" — minimal gear, own equipment + a few rentals
- "Full Package" — comprehensive rental from a rental house
- "Dream Package" — everything you'd want in an ideal world

Compare scenarios side-by-side to make informed decisions about your equipment budget.

## Adding Items

Within each scenario, add individual gear items with:

- **Name** — e.g. "Sony FX6", "Aputure 600d"
- **Category** — Camera, Lighting, Audio, Grip, etc.
- **Cost type** — per-day rental or flat fee
- **Cost amount** — the dollar amount

## Cost Models

Per-day items are multiplied by the number of shoot days they're assigned to, giving you an accurate total. Flat-fee items (purchases, weekly rentals) are counted once. The total cost per scenario is shown at a glance.

## Active Scenario

Mark one scenario as "active" — this is the package you've decided to go with. The active scenario's total cost rolls up into the Budget section under the "Gear" bucket.
