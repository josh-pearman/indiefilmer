## Crew Roster

The crew page lists everyone on your production team. Each entry shows the crew member's name, position (Director, DP, Gaffer, etc.), and contact info.

## Crew Details

Click a crew member to see their full profile:

- **Position** — their role on the production
- **Contact info** — phone, email
- **Emergency contact** — name and phone number
- **Dietary restrictions** — flows into catering planning
- **Day rate** — for budget tracking

## Shoot Day Assignments

Assign crew members to specific shoot days from either:

- The **crew member's detail page** — see all their assigned days
- The **shoot day detail page** — see all crew assigned to that day

Crew assignments drive two things: the crew list on call sheets and the headcount on catering pages.
