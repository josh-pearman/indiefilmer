## Cast List

The cast page lists all roles in your project with the assigned actor (if any), status, and contact info. This is your central hub for tracking who's playing what.

## Adding Cast Members

Click "Add Cast" to create a new role. You'll enter:

- **Role name** — the character name (e.g. "Detective Miller")
- **Actor name** — who's playing the role
- **Contact info** — phone, email
- **Dietary restrictions** — automatically shown on catering pages

## Casting Status

Track where you are in the casting process for each role:

- **Confirmed** — actor is locked in
- **Pending** — in discussions, awaiting confirmation
- **TBD** — role hasn't been cast yet
- **Backup** — alternate/understudy

## Rates & Budget

Set a day rate and estimated number of shoot days per cast member. These figures automatically roll up into the Budget section under the "Talent" bucket, so you always know your talent costs.
